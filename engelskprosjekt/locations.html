Airports and hotels

When you are gonna book a plane ticket you may have to take into consideration where you are gonna take the plane to. Three airports you could choose are, Heathrow, Gatwick and Stansted.

Here are some options of hotels you can book for that are near the centre of London If you want a little bit cheaper hotel you can get Hampton by Hilton London City or Hilton London Olympia If you want a luxury hotel you can book at London Hilton on Park Lane You can find more hotels at https://uk.hotels.com/

Resturants

There are multiple kinds of resturants in London. If you want to go to a Thai resturant you can go to The Great Thai Resturant, or if you want Italian you can go to La Goccia. If you want a fancy resturant you could go to Mount St. Resturant.

Activities

There are a few Tourist attractions you can go to in London, here are a few of them.

Big ben

Big ben or Elizabeth tower has a tour for everyone aged 11 and up where you can og inside the tower, Big ben is located at London SW1A 0AA, United Kingdom

London Eye

You can take the London Eye, a big ferris wheel where you can see almost the whole city from above. London Eye is located at Riverside Building, County Hall, London SE1 7PB, United Kingdom

Buckingham Palace

Buckingham palace is the official headquarter of the King, you can visit the main courtyard and look at tbe guards. The palace is located at London SW1A 1AA, United Kingdom

Football stadiums

There are a few football stadiums in London some of wich is Wembley Stadium, Tottenham Hotspur Stadium and Emirates Stadium You can visit these and watch a football match when there is one.

British Museum

The British Museum is a museum that has various items from all throughout history. The museum is located at Great Russell St, London WC1B 3DG, United Kingdom

Tower of London

Tower of London is a historical building in london it has existed for almost one tousand years, It holds the crown jewels and has previously been the royal palace and a prison

London Underground

The London Underground or The Tube is a underground metro system that can be very useful for getting around the place inside London.
